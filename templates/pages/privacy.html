Privacy Policy

Last updated: {% now "F Y" %}

At TEEBUSINESS, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the rights you have over your information.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly — such as your name, email, phone number and delivery address when you create an account, subscribe to our newsletter, place an order or contact us. We also collect limited technical data (such as your IP address and browser type) to keep your account secure and improve our service.

2. How we use your data

Your data is used to process and deliver orders, provide customer support, send verification and security notifications, and — only with your consent — share news about new arrivals, restocks and promotions. We never sell your personal data.

3. Legal basis (GDPR)

We process your data on the basis of contract (to fulfil your orders), legitimate interest (to secure and improve our service), and consent (for marketing emails, which you can withdraw at any time).

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you signed in, remember your cart and protect against fraud (CSRF). Optional analytics cookies help us understand how the store is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling essential cookies may affect core functionality.

5. Data security

We apply strong technical safeguards: encrypted password storage, secure sessions, CSRF and clickjacking protection, account-lockout against brute-force attacks, and HTTPS in production. Access to personal data is restricted to authorised staff only.

6. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, export or delete your personal data, and to unsubscribe from marketing at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

7. Data retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary to provide our services and meet legal obligations. When it is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymised.

8. Terms of use

By using this website you agree to use it lawfully and not to misuse our services or content. For full terms, please see our Terms & Conditions.
